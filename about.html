ABOUT

Kiminoyawa was founded as a quiet meeting point between contemporary experimentation and timeless composition. We curate immersive exhibitions that invite stillness, reflection, and conversation across cultures.

Our Vision

We believe art should be experienced slowly. Each exhibition is designed to guide visitors through a rhythm of contrast: material and light, silence and sound, intimacy and scale.

Curatorial Focus

Our programming highlights emerging and established voices in painting, sculpture, photography, and mixed media with a particular focus on works that connect Eastern sensibility and global contemporary practice.

The Space

Minimal architecture, natural textures, and controlled illumination shape a calm viewing atmosphere. The gallery is built to support both intimate pieces and large-scale installations.

A Place For Evening Harmony

Inspired by the name Kiminoyawa, "Your Evening Harmony", our gallery invites visitors to pause and reconnect with attention itself. Every show is curated not only to be seen, but to be felt over time.
